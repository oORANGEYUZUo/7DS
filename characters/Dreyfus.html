Dreyfus

SR
Description

A swordsmanship instructor in the Kingdom of Liones. In the past, he served as a Grandmaster of the Holy Knights, but spent ten years possessed by Fraudrin, a Demon. Now, he dedicates himself to training the next generation in the royal capital. He is the father of Griamore and grandfather of Dredrin.

Weapon Types: LongswordLanceRapier
