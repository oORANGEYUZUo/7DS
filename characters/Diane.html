Diane

SSR
Description

A member of <The Seven Deadly Sins> bearing the mark of Envy. In the past, she was falsely accused of treason and went into hiding, eventually settling in the Forest of White Dreams. She has a fondness for Meliodas, the leader of <The Seven Deadly Sins>.
