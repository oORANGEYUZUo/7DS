Gilthunder

SR
Description

A Holy Knight of the Kingdom of Liones, son of former Holy Knight Grandmaster Zaratras, and wielder of the magic of "Thunderbolt." He regrets his past as a traitor to the kingdom, a fate he endured to protect his beloved. Together with Howzer and Griamore, he is known as one of the Trio of Troublemakers.
