Tioreh

SSR
Description

The seventh child of King, the Fairy King, and Diane, the Giant Queen. Unable to resist her longing and curiosity of the outside world, she left the Fairy King's Forest to travel to the Kingdom of Liones. Though her boldness can sometimes cause misunderstandings, she possesses a warm and kind heart.
