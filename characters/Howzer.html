Howzer

SR
Description

The current Grandmaster of the Holy Knights of the Kingdom of Liones. His techniques use his magic of 「Tempest」. He was born a commoner but climbed the ranks to become the Grandmaster of the Holy Knights. He has a strong sense of duty to protect the kingdom, determined to safeguard its peace and honor the comrades who sacrificed themselves to protect it.
